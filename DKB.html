Who We Are:

The DANIEL ACADEMY is an Institution that believe that all young children need frequent opportunities to experience, learn from, and care for nature and the environment through high-quality education. The Alliance, a project of the North American Association for Environmental Education, serves as a backbone organization to focus and amplify the collective impact of the people and Institution that share this common vision.

What We Do:

DANIEL ACADEMY promotes quality and access to early childhood environmental education.
Specifically, we:
  1. Promote early childhood environmental education that is engaging, effective, safe, and inclusive.
  2. Increase the competencies of early childhood environmental educators to meet the needs of children with different abilities and from a diversity of backgrounds, including young people of color and those from underserved communities.
  3. Foster and support early childhood environmental education communities of practice.
  4. Advocate for policies that support nature-based early childhood education settings and programs.
  5. Promote research that informs early childhood environmental education practice and advocacy.
  6. Promote the value of early childhood environmental education to educators, policymakers, and parents.
